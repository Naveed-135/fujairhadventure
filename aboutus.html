Enjoy Adventure

Have A Fun With Our Amazing Camping

Welcome to the Reimagined Fujairah Adventure Park – the ultimate destination for thrill seekers and nature lovers alike. Discover our exciting attractions, including the UAE's tallest giant swing, thrilling zipline, and tranquil lakeside chalets. There's something for everyone, whether you're soaring through the skies or unwinding by the water. As the biggest adventure park in the area, we offer the perfect mix of excitement, fun, and unforgettable experiences. Plan your Adventure today and create memories that will last a lifetime.

The cost of camping really does depend on the location, time of year and the facilities on offer which can vary greatly. From nothing but an empty field and no running water or toilets, to holiday parks with full entertainment, kids club and swimming pool.
